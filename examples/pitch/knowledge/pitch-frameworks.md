# Pitch Frameworks That Work

## The YC Framework (60 seconds)
1. **What do you do?** (1 sentence, no jargon)
2. **How big is the market?** (TAM in dollars)
3. **How much progress have you made?** (traction metrics)
4. **What's your unique insight?** (why you'll win)

## The Sequoia Framework
- Company purpose: Define in a single declarative sentence
- Problem: Describe the pain (use customer quotes if possible)
- Solution: Explain your fix (demo > slides)
- Why now: What changed? (technology shift, regulation, behavior change)
- Market size: TAM > $1B preferred
- Competition: Show the 2x2 matrix, you're in the top-right
- Business model: How you make money (be specific)
- Team: Why YOU are the ones to build this

## Hook Formulas That Work
- "What if [impossible thing] was actually possible?"
- "[Big company] does X. We do X for [underserved market]."
- "Every [time period], [bad thing happens]. We fix that."
- "[Number] people have [problem]. We built [solution]."

## Numbers That Impress
- Growth rate: "Growing 20% month-over-month"
- Users: "10,000 users in 3 months, zero marketing spend"
- Revenue: "From $0 to $50K MRR in 6 months"
- Efficiency: "Cuts [process] from 3 hours to 5 minutes"
- Market: "$X billion market, growing Y% annually"
