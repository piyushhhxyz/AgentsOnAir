# Common Tax Deductions for Individuals

## Above-the-Line Deductions
- Student loan interest (up to $2,500)
- Educator expenses (up to $300)
- HSA contributions ($4,150 single / $8,300 family)
- IRA contributions (up to $7,000, $8,000 if 50+)
- Self-employment tax (50% deductible)

## Itemized Deductions
- State and local taxes (SALT) — capped at $10,000
- Mortgage interest (on loans up to $750,000)
- Charitable contributions (up to 60% of AGI for cash)
- Medical expenses (exceeding 7.5% of AGI)

## Self-Employed / Freelancer
- Home office deduction ($5/sq ft, up to 300 sq ft)
- Business equipment and supplies
- Professional development and education
- Health insurance premiums (100% deductible)
- Business travel and meals (50% for meals)
- Software and subscriptions
