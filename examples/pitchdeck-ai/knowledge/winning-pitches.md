# Winning Pitch Examples

## Airbnb (YC W09)
"Book rooms with locals, rather than hotels."
- Problem: Hotels are expensive and impersonal
- Solution: Marketplace for people to list and book unique spaces
- Hook: "We let you stay in someone's home — and both of you love it"
- Result: $100B+ company

## Dropbox (YC S07)
"Your files, anywhere."
- Problem: USB drives, email attachments, lost files
- Solution: A folder that syncs everywhere
- Hook: "It just works. Put a file in the folder, it's everywhere."
- Result: $10B+ company

## Stripe (YC S09)
"Payments for developers."
- Problem: Accepting payments online requires weeks of integration
- Solution: 7 lines of code to accept payments
- Hook: "We're the AWS of payments — 7 lines of code, you're live"
- Result: $95B company

## What Makes These Work
1. **Simplicity**: One sentence explains the whole thing
2. **Specificity**: Real numbers, not vague claims
3. **Analogy**: "X for Y" format (Uber for X, Airbnb for Y)
4. **Contrast**: Before vs. after (3 hours → 5 minutes)
5. **Confidence**: No hedging, no "we think", no "maybe"
