# Cold VC Outreach Playbook

## Subject Line Formulas (aim for <6 words)
- "[Metric] in [timeframe]" → "4x revenue in 90 days"
- "[Company] + [their portfolio co]" → "Stripe for healthcare"
- "[Specific reference]" → "Re: your AI agents tweet"
- "Quick question, [first name]" → personal, curiosity-driven
- "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out" → warm intro substitute

## Email Structure (The 4-Line Framework)
1. **Hook** (1 line): Reference something specific about them
2. **Traction** (1-2 lines): Hard numbers that prove momentum
3. **Why you** (1 line): Unique insight or unfair advantage
4. **Ask** (1 line): Specific, low-friction next step

## Example — The Warm Intro Substitute
Subject: $2M ARR, 40% MoM — 15 min?

Loved your take on vertical AI at the Cerebral Valley event.

We're building [one-liner]. $2M ARR, 40% month-over-month growth,
80 enterprise customers including [notable name].

I spent 8 years at [relevant company] — saw this gap firsthand.

Worth 15 minutes Tuesday or Wednesday?

## Follow-Up Sequence
- **Day 0**: Initial email (Tuesday/Wednesday 8-10am their timezone)
- **Day 3**: Reply to same thread — add one new data point
- **Day 7**: Forward original with "Bumping this — [new milestone]"
- **Day 14**: Final touch — different angle or mutual connection
- **After Day 14**: Move on. Don't be desperate.

## Personalization Research Checklist
Before writing any email, find:
- [ ] VC's recent tweets or blog posts (last 30 days)
- [ ] Their latest investment and why they made it
- [ ] Portfolio companies similar to yours (synergy angle)
- [ ] Any public talks, podcasts, or interviews
- [ ] Mutual connections (LinkedIn 2nd degree)
- [ ] Their stated thesis on your market/category

## Metrics That Matter to VCs (by stage)
### Pre-Seed / Seed
- Waitlist size, LOIs, pilot customers
- Founder background / domain expertise
- Speed of iteration / shipping velocity

### Series A
- ARR ($1-3M), growth rate (3x+ YoY)
- Net revenue retention (>120%)
- CAC payback period (<12 months)
- Logo quality (recognizable customers)

### Series B+
- ARR ($10M+), path to profitability
- Market share and competitive moat
- Unit economics at scale
- Expansion revenue
